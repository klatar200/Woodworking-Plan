Steps

2 Assemble Legs

Tools Drill Kreg Jig
Materials 2×2 legs 1×3×14″ boards

After drilling your pocket holes (as shown), attach your 2″×2″ legs via your 1″×3″×14″ boards. Start by attaching the top and bottom boards, then add the middle support. Check that each leg assembly sits square before moving on — a racked pair here will fight you for the rest of the build.
