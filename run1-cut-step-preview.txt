--- 1x3-blanket-ladder
Cut two rails at 72" and five rungs at 16" from the 1x3 furring strips. All of these come off the same 3/4" x 2-1/2" stock, so only the lengths differ. Those pieces take three 8 ft boards once you allow about 1/8" for each saw cut. Square every cut and label each piece as it comes off the saw.
    tools: [tape-measure,circular-saw]  materials: [1x3 furring strips, 8 ft]
