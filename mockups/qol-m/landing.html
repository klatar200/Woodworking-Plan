What sets it apart

Every claim here is something the app does today.

The cut-list optimizer turns a plan's parts into how many boards of what size to buy — accounting for saw kerf and ripping narrow parts from wider stock.

Tick your tools once and see only the plans you can actually build — essential tools only.

Built for the shop

Who it's for

For the “what can I build this weekend?” woodworker.

You've got a Saturday, a pile of 2×4s, and a tape measure. You don't want a PDF with the cut list buried on page 7 — you want to know what to build and what to buy. That's the whole idea.

  • Save plans and roll materials into one shopping list.
  • Log your builds and leave a review with photos.
  • Free, and it works with one bar of signal in the shop.
Before you ask

Quick answers

Is it free?

Yes — free to browse and use, with no ads and no affiliate links.

Do I need an account?

No account to browse. One's only needed to save plans, build a shopping list, or log a build.

Does it work offline?

Yes — install it to your phone and your saved plans, cut lists, and shopping list work with no signal.
